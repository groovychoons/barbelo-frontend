Barbelo was formed in 2017 by 9 Cardiff Uni students... Barbelo is a platform for social change, education liberation, poverty alleviation and most importantly, for the cause that matters most to you. Barbelo will exist to bring dreams into fruition and funds into reach, with our core aim being focused on connecting angels from across the globe to help those who need it most.
Barbelo has no platform fees and it never will; by removing platform fees, we ensure that all of the money raised goes directly to the real person behind the story — that’s why the story is so important to us.
By focusing on the story behind a project, we provide angels with an insight into every detail. The reason this approach is so fundamentally important is that it allows people to connect. We live in an age where the physical boundaries that once separated us no longer apply, however, our natural inquisition to get to know a person will never change. When you know someone, you build a relationship, and thus you are more inclined to provide your assistance in any way that you can.
We are not a charity, but instead, a platform that provides a compassionate approach to crowdfunding. We think that the all-or-nothing funding model that has become the industry standard is fundamentally broken, as it does not allow you to invest in the things that matter most to people.
We want to build communities centred around projects, to encourage project creators to get to know their angels and to show you that your investment, no matter how small, can make all the difference.
